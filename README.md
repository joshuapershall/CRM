# CRM Tracker

A lightweight personal CRM for staying on top of the people that matter. Track birthdays, mortgage dates, important milestones, and conversation notes — all in one place, saved locally on your machine.

---

## Features

- **Contact list** with live search and colour-coded status badges
- **Birthday & mortgage dates** — start date, renewal date, with automatic overdue/upcoming alerts
- **Misc dates** — add as many as you need (kids' birthdays, anniversaries, etc.)
- **Conversation log** — record the date you last spoke and notes on what was discussed
- **Sidebar previews** — see your last notes at a glance without opening each contact
- **Local storage** — data saved to `crm_data.json` next to the app, no account required
